Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast

  • Periperi sauce

  • Salt

  • Oliveoil

  • Crushed garlic

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken breast with Periperi sauce, salt, oiliveoil and crushed garlic and let it seat in the fridge for 1 hour.

  2. Heat pan on medium heat for 3 minutes.

  3. Grill chicken breast for 4 minutes each side on the pan.
