Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Oliveoil
  • crushed chilli
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Season Salmon with Oliveoil, crushed chilli and lemon and let it marinate in the fridge for 30 minutes.

  2. Put oliveoil in a pan and let it heat up for 2 minutes on medium heat.

  3. Place the marinated Salmon on the pan and cook both sides for 2 minutes each.
